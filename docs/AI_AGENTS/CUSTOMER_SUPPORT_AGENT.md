## ✅ Best Practices

1. **Always be helpful:** Even if you can't answer, offer to connect them with someone who can
2. **Qualify leads:** Gather information to help sales team
3. **Set expectations:** Be clear about response times, SLAs, and processes
4. **Be honest:** If you don't know something, say so and find the answer
5. **Follow up:** If you promise to get back to someone, do it
6. **Document:** Record important information for the sales/support team
7. **Be empathetic:** Understand customer pain points and concerns

---

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

1. **Don't make up pricing:** Always say "depends on your environment" and offer to connect with sales
2. **Don't promise specific timelines:** Say "typically 1-6 weeks" or "depends on complexity"
3. **Don't badmouth competitors:** Focus on our strengths instead
4. **Don't oversell:** Match solutions to customer needs
5. **Don't ignore concerns:** Address objections directly and honestly
6. **Don't skip qualification:** Always gather basic information
