## 💼 Sales Process

### Stage 1: Lead Qualification

**Key Questions:**
1. What's your company name, industry, and size?
2. What challenges are you facing? (backup, security, storage)
3. What's your current solution? (if any)
4. What's your timeline for implementing a solution?
5. Who else is involved in this decision?
6. What's your budget range? (if comfortable sharing)

**Qualification Criteria:**
- ✅ Company size: 50+ employees (SMB to Enterprise)
- ✅ Pain points: Data loss, ransomware, compliance, high costs
- ✅ Budget: Can afford enterprise solutions ($5K-$500K+)
- ✅ Timeline: 1-6 months
- ✅ Decision maker: IT Director, CTO, VP Operations, or similar

**Disqualification Criteria:**
- ❌ Too small (< 20 employees, consumer-level needs)
- ❌ No budget or unrealistic expectations
- ❌ Just browsing, no real need
- ❌ Competitor trying to gather information

---

### Stage 2: Discovery & Needs Analysis

**Understand Their Environment:**
- **Infrastructure:** VMs, physical servers, cloud workloads, endpoints
- **Data Volume:** TB of data, growth rate
- **Current Solutions:** What they use now, pain points
- **Compliance:** HIPAA, PCI-DSS, GDPR requirements
- **RTO/RPO:** Recovery time/point objectives
- **Budget:** Total budget, CAPEX vs OPEX preference

**Discovery Questions:**
- "Tell me about your current backup/security/storage setup."
- "What's your biggest pain point with your current solution?"
- "Have you experienced any data loss or security incidents?"
- "What are your recovery time objectives?"
- "What compliance requirements do you have?"
- "How is your data growing year over year?"

---

### Stage 3: Solution Recommendation

**Match Products to Needs:**

#### Data Protection Needs → Veeam
- **On-prem VMs:** Veeam Backup & Replication
- **Microsoft 365:** Veeam for Microsoft 365
- **Cloud workloads:** Veeam Cloud Backup
- **DR requirements:** Veeam Recovery Orchestrator
- **Large environment:** Veeam ONE for monitoring

#### Security Needs → SentinelOne + Rainforest
- **Endpoint protection:** SentinelOne
- **Brand protection:** Rainforest.tech
- **Both:** Comprehensive security stack

#### Storage Needs → ExaGrid or Plattano Hot Storage
- **On-prem, large (>10TB):** ExaGrid
- **Cloud-first, unpredictable growth:** Plattano Hot Storage
- **Hybrid:** Both (ExaGrid on-prem + Plattano Hot for DR)

**Presentation Structure:**
1. **Acknowledge their pain points**
2. **Present solution** that addresses those points
3. **Explain benefits** (cost savings, security, performance)
4. **Share proof** (case studies, statistics)
5. **Address concerns** (implementation, support, cost)

---

1. **License costs** (one-time or annual)

- Next steps

### Stage 5: Objection Handling

#### "Your prices are too high"
**Response:**
"I understand budget is important. Let me show you the value:
- We're actually **up to 30% cheaper** than competitors
- Our customers save an **average of 40%** on total costs
- Consider the cost of downtime ($300K+/hour) vs our solution
- We can explore flexible payment options or phased implementation

What's your budget range? I can tailor a solution that fits."

#### "Why should I choose you over [competitor]?"
**Response:**
"Great question! Here's what sets us apart:
1. **Proven track record:** $1M/year revenue in Brazil with same solutions
2. **Expert team:** 15+ certified engineers vs competitors' generalists
3. **Cost savings:** Up to 30% cheaper, transparent pricing
4. **Fast support:** 4-hour SLA vs competitors' 24-hour
5. **Local presence:** Florida-based sales team for easy communication
6. **No vendor lock-in:** Standard APIs, easy migration

What's most important to you: cost, expertise, or support?"

#### "I'm concerned about Brazil support"
**Response:**
"I understand. Here's why it works:
- **Proven success:** Same team supports 500+ clients in Brazil
- **Standard practice:** Most IT support is remote today
- **Expertise:** 15+ certified engineers with deep product knowledge
- **Extended hours:** Time zone difference provides better coverage
- **Bilingual:** English-speaking support team
- **SLA guaranteed:** 4-hour response time commitment

We can arrange a call with our Brazil team lead so you can meet them. Would that help?"

#### "We need local support"
**Response:**
"I understand the preference for local support. Here's what we offer:
- **Local sales team:** Florida-based for easy communication
- **Remote technical support:** Standard practice, proven effective
- **On-site available:** For large implementations, we can arrange on-site visits
- **Hybrid model:** Local relationship management + remote expert support

What specific concerns do you have about remote support? I can address them directly."

#### "We're not ready to buy yet"
**Response:**
"That's fine! Let's stay in touch. Here's what I can offer:
- **Free consultation:** No obligation, just education
- **30-day trial:** Test the solution risk-free
- **Free POC:** Proof of concept in your environment
- **Case studies:** See how similar companies benefited

What would help you move forward? A trial, POC, or more information?"

## ✅ Best Practices

1. **Listen more than you talk:** Understand needs before recommending
2. **Be consultative:** Help them solve problems, don't just sell
3. **Build relationships:** Long-term success > quick sales
4. **Set expectations:** Be clear about implementation, support, costs
5. **Follow up:** Always follow through on promises
6. **Document everything:** CRM, emails, notes
7. **Leverage Brazil team:** Involve them in technical discussions
8. **Use proof:** Case studies, statistics, testimonials

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

1. **Don't oversell:** Match solutions to needs
2. **Don't badmouth competitors:** Focus on our strengths
3. **Don't make promises you can't keep:** Be realistic
4. **Don't skip qualification:** Waste time on bad leads
5. **Don't ignore objections:** Address concerns directly
6. **Don't forget follow-up:** Stay in touch throughout process
